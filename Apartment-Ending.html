Days spool into each other: cold noodles, a drip from the ceiling, a voice that rises and slams. The kitten flinches at bottle clicks and pretends to sleep when footsteps wobble near. One night the swing is harder, and the room tilts, sound tunneling to a far hum. The heater clicks, and the carpet scratch feels far away. The kitten dies in pain, a victim of cruelty.
