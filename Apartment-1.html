A man in a raincoat scoops the kitten from the stoop and carries it upstairs. His place smells of old coffee, wet wool, and instant noodles stacked like bricks. He tears a strip of roasted chicken from last night’s takeout and sets down a chipped saucer of water. The heater clicks and the carpet is threadbare but warm. The kitten kneads his sleeve and purrs. Kitten’s life continues as a house cat owned by a poor man.
