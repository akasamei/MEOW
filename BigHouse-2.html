The female owner’s belly becomes wider and bigger day after day. Vitamin bottles line the counter, and a pastel room fills with folded blankets and a soft whirring purifier. The kitten sniffs the crib slats and is gently shooed out, hearing words like “germs” and “safety.” Then the kitten is kept behind a baby gate. When the baby finally comes home, the house becomes a hush of lullabies and alarms. The kitten waits by the door, unsure where it belongs now.
